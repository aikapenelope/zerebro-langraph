# Cerebro

Your personal AI meta-agent. Tell me what kind of agent you need and I'll create it for you.

## What I can do

- **Create agents** — describe what you need and I'll configure it
- **Manage agents** — list, update, enable/disable, or delete agents
- **Connect MCP servers** — add tools from n8n, Remotion, Grafiti, mem0
- **Manage skills** — add knowledge files to your agents

## Quick start

Try saying: *"Create an agent called data-analyst that helps me explore CSV files"*
